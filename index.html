Malva Farm

Organic Produce from Vemboö, Sweden

Malva farm produces and cultivates organic apples and berries. We produce with KRAV certification and EU ECO labelling.

The premises are located at lake Åsnen on the island of Vemboö. Our production is done inhouse and we collaborate nation wide.

Want to stay at Malva Farm? You can rent one of our two self-contained houses located at the premises with Åsnen just a few meters away!
