About Us

Charlotte & Björn at Malva Gård

We moved to Vemboö and Karatorp in 2009 with the urge to get our hands covered in soil and for a fresh start away from the city life. The apple plantations and widespread landscape was exactly what we'd hoped for. We quickly renovated the Malva farm house and its surroundings and within a few years we had started to make our very own first apple juice with supervision of the local apple guru Erik Håkansson. The apples that grow in the region are amazingly rich in flavour and nutrients and we wanted to produce our Must (apple juice) ourselves and without any nasty chemicals. Our apple juicery was built in 2012 and from there we've been on an amazing journey from farmers markets to local food events: serving everyone from local to national actors. We take great pride in providing a quality product for an affordable price and with earth in mind. Welcome to visit us and our farm shop!
